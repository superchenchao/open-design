# Silicon Valley 词表

来源: TV-drama vocab learning prototype  
整理时间: 2026-05-26

| # | Word / Phrase | POS | Episode | Tag | 中文解释 | English |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | pre-money valuation | N. | S1 E01 | VC | 投前估值。投资人打钱进来之前,公司值多少; 投后估值 = 投前 + 本轮融资额。 | A company's value before new investment money is added. |
| 2 | burn rate | N. | S1 E04 | VC | 烧钱速度。公司每月净流出现金,配合现金余额就能算出 runway。 | How fast a startup spends its cash on hand each month. |
| 3 | runway | N. | S1 E04 | VC | 现金跑道。按当前烧钱速度还能撑几个月; 跑道越长,谈判越从容。 | How many months you can keep operating before running out of cash. |
| 4 | term sheet | N. | S1 E04 | VC | 投资条款书。还没签字的草案,但估值、股权、清算优先权等关键条款都已敲定。 | A non-binding draft of the headline terms of an investment. |
| 5 | fiduciary duty | N. | S1 E04 | VC | 受托责任。拿了投资人的钱后,在法律上必须为股东利益做决定。 | A legal obligation to act in the best interest of another party, here shareholders. |
| 6 | vesting cliff | N. | S1 E04 | VC | 股权悬崖。必须干满一定时间,通常一年,才解锁第一批股权。 | A minimum tenure before any stock vests, usually one year. |
| 7 | third comma | N. | S1 E05 | show coinage | 第三个逗号。Russ Hanneman 用来指“十亿美元身家”的说法。 | Russ Hanneman's shorthand for being a billionaire: three commas in your net worth. |
| 8 | this guy fucks | phrase | S1 E04 | show coinage | Russ Hanneman 的粗口式最高肯定,大致是“这哥们儿是个狠人”。职场慎用。 | Russ Hanneman's highest praise, roughly “this guy is the real deal.” |
| 9 | middle-out | ADJ. | S1 E08 | show coinage | 中间向外。剧中虚构的压缩算法核心思想。 | Pied Piper's fictional compression breakthrough: process from the middle outwards. |
| 10 | Weissman score | N. | S1 E08 | show coinage | Weissman 评分。压缩比 + 速度的综合分,剧组请 Stanford 教授帮忙设计的虚构基准。 | A fictional benchmark for compression algorithms, co-designed for the show with a real Stanford professor. |
| 11 | tip-to-tip efficiency | N. | S1 E08 | show coinage | 剧中后台白板笑点,也是 middle-out 灵感来源。慎用于正经场合。 | Erlich's infamous backstage thought experiment about maximum throughput. |
| 12 | TechCrunch Disrupt | N. | S1 E08 | tech | TechCrunch 主办的创业大赛/展会,剧中 Pied Piper 在这里赢了第一名。 | A real annual startup competition run by TechCrunch. |
| 13 | bridge round | N. | S2 | VC | 过桥融资。在两轮正式融资之间先借一笔小钱续命。 | A short-term funding round to keep a startup alive between two larger rounds. |
| 14 | down round | N. | S2 | VC | 估值下跌的融资轮。新一轮估值比上一轮更低。 | A funding round priced lower than the previous one. |
| 15 | liquidation preference | N. | S2 E03 | VC | 清算优先权。公司被卖掉时,投资人优先拿回本金或倍数。 | A clause that lets investors get their money back first if the company is sold or wound down. |
| 16 | cap table | N. | S2 | VC | 股权结构表。列出谁持有多少股、什么类型、行权价是多少。 | A spreadsheet showing every shareholder, share count, and class. |
| 17 | dilution | N. | S2 | VC | 股权稀释。公司发新股后,你原有的持股百分比被摊薄。 | The drop in your ownership percentage when new shares are issued. |
| 18 | NDA | N. | S2 | office speak | 保密协议。Non-Disclosure Agreement, 约定不外传机密信息。 | Non-Disclosure Agreement: a contract not to share confidential information. |

| 21 | tabs vs spaces | N. phrase | S3 E06 | tech | 程序员经典圣战:缩进用 Tab 还是 Space。 | The eternal programmer debate over code indentation. |
| 22 | enterprise | ADJ. / N. | S3 | tech | 面向大公司的产品。合同大、销售周期长、要求稳定性。 | Aimed at big-company customers: large contracts, long sales cycles, stability over flash. |
| 23 | SaaS | N. | S3 | tech | Software as a Service。软件按月订阅,部署在云上。 | Software delivered as a monthly cloud subscription. |

| 25 | ramen profitable | ADJ. phrase | S4 | VC | 泡面级盈利。公司赚的钱刚好够创始人低成本活下去。 | Earning just enough to feed the founders instant noodles. |
| 26 | moat | N. | S4 | VC | 护城河。公司难以被竞争对手复制的壁垒。 | A defensible advantage that keeps competitors out. |
| 27 | 10x engineer | N. phrase | S4 | show coinage | 十倍工程师。指生产力远超平均水平的程序员,剧中常被自封。 | A controversial industry term for a programmer claimed to be ten times as productive as average. |
| 28 | brogrammer | N. | S4 | office speak | Bro + programmer。健身房、兄弟会、写代码型工程师,带贬义。 | A programmer who acts like a frat bro. |
| 29 | DAU | N. | S4 | tech | Daily Active Users。日活用户,增长团队常盯的指标。 | Daily Active Users, a core engagement metric for consumer apps. |
| 30 | virality coefficient | N. | S4 | tech | 病毒系数。平均每个用户能拉来多少新用户; k > 1 就会指数增长。 | How many new users each existing user brings. |
| 31 | decentralized internet | N. phrase | S5 E01 | tech | 去中心化互联网。不依赖大公司服务器,数据分散存在用户设备上。 | A peer-to-peer internet that does not rely on centralized corporate servers. |
| 32 | peer-to-peer | ADJ. | S5 | tech | 点对点。节点之间直接通信,不经过中心服务器。 | A network architecture where nodes talk directly instead of through a central server. |
| 33 | node | N. | S5 | tech | 节点。网络里参与通信和存储的一台设备或一个进程。 | A single device or process participating in a network. |
| 34 | blockchain | N. | S5 | tech | 区块链。由密码学链接的分布式账本。 | A cryptographically chained distributed ledger. |
| 35 | protocol | N. | S5 | tech | 协议。机器之间约定好怎么通信的规则。 | The agreed-upon rules for how machines communicate. |
| 36 | AI ethics | N. phrase | S6 | tech | AI 伦理。AI 系统该不该做、做错了谁负责。 | The moral questions around what AI systems should do and who is accountable. |
| 37 | end-to-end encryption | N. phrase | S6 | tech | 端到端加密。只有发送方和接收方能解密,中间节点也看不到内容。 | A scheme where only the sender and recipient can read a message. |
| 38 | acqui-hire | N. | S6 | VC | 收购 + 雇佣。大公司收购小公司主要为了团队而不是产品。 | Buying a startup primarily for its team rather than its product. |
| 39 | exit strategy | N. phrase | S6 | VC | 退出策略。投资人如何收回回报:IPO、被收购或卖二手股。 | How investors plan to cash out: IPO, acquisition, or secondary sale. |
